Proofs

Let's prove that 3+3=7!

Proof 1

3+3=6, as you know, and 3+3=8, because you can make a shape "8" when you use two "3"s. So, in average, 3+3 should be 7.

Proof 2

42 is the answer of everything, so (3+3)*(3+3)=42. 3+3=6, so 6*(3+3)=42. Therefore, 3+3=7.
